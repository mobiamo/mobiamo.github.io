About product

Who We are

Mobiamo – mobile payment system allowing merchants to currently accept payments in 65 countries. Mobiamo is a mobile payments platform developed by Paymentwall with a global network of more than 150 mobile carriers. It allows merchants to accept mobile payments for digital goods and services through premium SMS billing, mobile subscriptions, direct carrier and WAP billing. Mobiamo can be used in mobile apps and games, free-to-play games, in-app purchases. With its software development kit (SDK), developers can integrate Mobiamo in both iOS and Android apps.

What we do

Mobiamo provides the latest mobile payment solutions including premium SMS billing, mobile subscriptions, direct carrier-billing and WAP billing to allow users to easily and securely pay for web or mobile content.

Why Choose Us

Mobiamo has announced independence from Paymentwall in October 2015 and is now operating as an independent company. The separation will allow Mobiamo to enhance its management process and become more flexible in its operations.
